Hi! I am Disha Deshpande, instagram name the_canon_girl. I am from Mumbai, India. Photography has been my passion and I am perseuing studies for the same. I am an independant girl learning through all those little experiences. That's the essence of life for me. Experiences help us learn and improve.

Beleiving in difference of perception, I love to capture my vision using my camera. Hundreds and thousands of things happen around us each minute, and each of those actions can be captured as a photograph. For me, photography has always been about expressing my vision. Likes or followings on instagram really doesn't matter so far as my message is portrayed and understood correctly. I want to put my view in front of the world so as for them to be able to see things from a different angle.

Just like dance and music is a means of expression for people, my photography is that means to me. It is an initiative to make photography acceptable to people specially in India. Photography is an art. One can not just click and become a photographer!
